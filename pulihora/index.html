Tamarind Rice (Pulihora)

A tangy, spicy, and aromatic staple from South India.

Ingredients

Base

  • Rice: 2 cups (cooked and cooled)
  • Tamarind: Small lemon-sized ball (soaked in water)
  • Turmeric: 1/2 tsp
  • Salt: To taste

Tempering (Popu)

  • Oil: 3 tbsp
  • Mustard seeds: 1 tsp
  • Chana dal & Urad dal: 1 tsp each
  • Dried red chilies: 3-4
  • Green chilies: 2-3 (slit)
  • Curry leaves: 1 sprig
  • Peanuts: 2 tbsp

Instructions

  1. Extract the thick tamarind pulp, add salt and turmeric, and boil until thickened.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add peanuts and dals until golden brown.
  3. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and chilies to the pan.
  4. Mix the tamarind paste into the tempering, then fold into the cooked rice.
Pro Tip: Let the Pulihora rest for at least 30 minutes before serving; the flavors absorb much better!
